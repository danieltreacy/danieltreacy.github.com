About myself

  • I was born in Melbourne, Australia to an American mother and Australian father. I currently live in Sydney.
  • I hold a US passport. After I was born my grandmother encouraged my mother to get me registered as a citizen born abroad. While I grew up in Australia and consider myself an Australian, I lived and travelled in America for 3 years, and am proud to be a US citizen.
  • I work for Maxus, a media agency, as a software engineer and technologist. I'm having a blast working with some very talented people in what I feel is a very exciting industry ripe for technological innovation. Check out my LinkedIn profile for my work history.
  • While I may not be a stereotypical nerd, I love programming and building systems that do amazing things. My first language was PHP, and my first professional language was Java. While tedious and verbose, Java is an extremely mature language (developmentally speaking) and the JVM is a workhorse platform worthy of any production server. I learnt a lot from Java. I also started dabbling in Scala and want to look into other JVM languages in the future. My current job involves a lot of Ruby, and I use it as my primary scripting language, for prototyping, and for most of my own browser-based projects. I am not a fan of mobile web frameworks as they don't give the user the best possible experience for their hardware. I'm keenly awaiting the day when that changes.
  • I also play piano, keyboard, and organ and have done since I was five. I love writing music, and can lose touch with reality for hours on end playing and composing. I have owned acoustic pianos, organs, and keyboards, but my current partner in crime is a Korg M50 synthesizer workstation which satisfies my every need.
  • In addition to writing my own music, I am a huge appreciator of others' music. I am generally quite eclectic as far as genres are concerned, but my favourites are electro, indie rock and pop, folk, all kinds of jazz, and have recently acquired a taste for experimental and ambient music. Good remixes or covers make my spine tingle.
  • I got married in 2010 to a woman I don't deserve, and I consider myself a happy man.
