History

Right now I'm working as a software engineer for MLC, the investment banking arm of the National Australia Bank. I started there in February 2010 as an intern, and after 10 months they offered me a position as a contractor. It's a great place to work overall, though like any enterprise has its fare share of challenges. What makes MLC different, I believe, is that they have technical minded-people in key leadership positions, enabling them to continue to invest in their online strategy.

Prior to MLC, I was freelancing as a web designer/developer through my solo agency CreativON. I still do a little bit of freelancing on the side, but it is not my main focus, and I'm trying to move away from thin-client front-end design.

My first real experience was as an intern at a web design agency, Shifted Pixels. It was my first year of university and I wanted to ditch my retail job and find something more in line with my career. It was there I met Nick Holmes a Court who was a partner there, and effectively, my boss. I really appreciated the short but meaningful experience I gained working at Shifted, and I continue to keep in touch with Nick and the great work he's doing in his social media monitoring startup, BuzzNumbers.
