I love discovering new programming languages and frameworks, though I usually will develop in the JVM (Java, Scala) to pay the bills, and Ruby for fun or side projects.

I am an avid book lover and while partial to science fiction and fantasy (Tolkien, Orson Scott Card, Asimov), I also enjoy the occasional classic Dickens or Austen. I also have a healthy appetite for non-fiction, particularly about business, and especialy those challenging the traditional product or marketing function (see 'Purple Cow' by Seth Godin).

I love playing piano and keyboards, and have been since I was 5 years old. I write music occasionally, but tend to keep it to myself. Hopefully some day I can devote more time to it and share it with the world.
